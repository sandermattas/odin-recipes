Banana Pankcakes

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • A handful of chopped fresh parsley
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat the olive oil over medium heat in a large pan.
  3. Add the garlic and chili flakes, cook until the garlic is golden brown.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving some of the pasta water.
  5. Add the spaghetti to the pan and toss well. If needed, add a little pasta water.
  6. erve with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese.
