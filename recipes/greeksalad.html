Greek Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 200g feta cheese, diced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cucumber, tomatoes, onion, feta, and olives.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and mix gently to combine.
  4. Sprinkle with oregano before serving.
